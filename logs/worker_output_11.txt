Thought: To calculate the final price of a product that costs $1000 with a 10% discount, we need to apply the discount to the original price. This involves a mathematical operation.

Action: Use the code_tool to perform the calculation.

Action Input: 
```python
# Define the original price and discount percentage
original_price = 1000
discount_percentage = 0.10

# Calculate the discount amount
discount_amount = original_price * discount_percentage

# Calculate the final price by subtracting the discount from the original price
final_price = original_price - discount_amount

print(final_price)
```
