Thought: To calculate the final price of a product after a 10% discount, we first need to find the discount amount by multiplying the original price by the discount percentage, and then subtract this amount from the original price.

Action: Compute the final price using the formula: Final Price = Original Price - (Original Price * Discount Percentage)

Action Input: 
Original Price = $1000
Discount Percentage = 10% or 0.10

Using the code_tool to perform the computation:

```python
original_price = 1000
discount_percentage = 0.10
discount_amount = original_price * discount_percentage
final_price = original_price - discount_amount
print("The final price is: ", final_price)
```
