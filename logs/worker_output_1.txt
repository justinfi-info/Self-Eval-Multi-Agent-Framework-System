Thought: To calculate the final price of a product with a 10% discount, we first need to find the discount amount by multiplying the initial price by the discount percentage, and then subtract the discount amount from the initial price.

Action: Compute the discount amount and final price
Action Input: 
Initial price = $1000, Discount percentage = 10% or 0.10

Using code_tool to perform the computation:

```python
# Define variables
initial_price = 1000
discount_percentage = 0.10

# Calculate discount amount
discount_amount = initial_price * discount_percentage

# Calculate final price
final_price = initial_price - discount_amount

print("The final price is: ", final_price)
```

Final Answer: $900
