Thought: To calculate the final price of a product after a 10% discount, we need to first find the discount amount by multiplying the original price by the discount percentage, then subtract this amount from the original price.

Action: Compute the final price using the code_tool.

Action Input: 
```python
# Define variables
original_price = 1000
discount_percentage = 0.10

# Calculate discount amount
discount_amount = original_price * discount_percentage

# Calculate final price
final_price = original_price - discount_amount

print(final_price)
```
